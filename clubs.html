CC club

CC Club (Creative Coders Club) is a student-led community that brings together tech enthusiasts who are passionate about coding, problem-solving, and building innovative projects. The club hosts coding challenges, workshops, hackathons, and project showcases to help members grow their technical skills and collaborate with like-minded peers. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, CC Club is the perfect place to learn, share, and create together.
